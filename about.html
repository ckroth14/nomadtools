About Nomad Tools

Built by a tech who does the work. Designed to solve the problems that show up every day on a job site.

Where this came from

nomad.poe started as a field problem. Every camera install hits the same wall — you need power and a network to aim, focus, and configure the camera, but neither exists yet at the point of installation.

The existing tools — Veracity, Axis — solve part of the problem, but they're outdated hardware at a premium price. Proprietary batteries. 10/100 ethernet. No software integration.

nomad.poe runs on the batteries already on your truck. Gigabit ethernet. Built-in Wi-Fi router. Pairs with free software. $299.

The product line

nomad is a brand for field tools built around a consistent workflow. nomad.poe is the first device. nomad.ip ToolBox is the first software. More tools are in development.

The goal is a platform that reduces the time, equipment, and friction involved in camera and network installations — tools that a tech reaches for on every job.

Production status

nomad.poe is a working prototype in limited production. Units are being built and tested. The waitlist is open. If you're a company looking to put these in your techs' hands, get in touch directly.
