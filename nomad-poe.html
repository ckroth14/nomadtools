What it does

⚡

Battery powered

Runs on Makita or Milwaukee 18V tool batteries. The ones already on your truck. Swap in a fresh battery and keep going.

📡

PoE+ output

Full 802.3at PoE+ at ~30W. Powers IP cameras, access control readers, and other PoE devices over a single cable.

🌐

Built-in router

Creates its own local network with DHCP, a Wi-Fi access point, and wired Ethernet. No customer network needed.

📷

Camera setup

Aim, focus, and configure cameras before infrastructure exists. Access camera web interfaces from your phone or laptop.

📱

Phone tethering

USB port supports phone tethering for internet access in the field — useful for firmware updates or remote support.

🎒

Grab and go

Shoulder strap included. Shows up on a job, does its job, goes back in the bag. No setup, no teardown.
